How It Works

Get started in minutes

Three simple steps to transform your group shopping and spending experience.

1

Create Your Group

Sign up, create a group, and invite your roommates, family, or friends with a simple invite code. Admin approval keeps it secure.

2

Build Your List

Add items manually, use voice commands, paste a recipe URL, import from a shared receipt, or let AI build your list. Everyone can contribute in real-time.

3

Shop & Split
